HOW TO MAKE PIZZAAAA


This is a pizza, the worlds best comfort food

This food was invented to help you enjoy your next binge tv series

Ingredients

  • 1 14,5 ounce can peeled tomatoes undrained
  • 1/2 Teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt
  • 11 Pround package refrigerated pizza dough
  • 8 Ounces fresh mozzarella sliced
  • 8 Fresh basil leaves, torn

This is how you make it dough... haha

  1. Heat oven to 450*F and in a small saucepan, on medium heat
    Combine the tomatoes and their juices, the oregano and 1/4 teaspoon salt.
    Bring to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and simmer, crushing the tomatoes with a wooden
    spoon until the sauve thickens, 10 to 15mins.
  3. Shape the dough into one 12 inch round and place on baking sheet
    Cover with the sauce and top with grated cheese.
  4. Bake until the crust is golden brown and crisp, around 20 mins.
  5. Transfer to a cutting board and sprinkle with the basil. Slive and
    divide among individual plates.
